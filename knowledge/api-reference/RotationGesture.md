# RotationGesture

**Namespace:** `Tizen.NUI`
**Inherits:** `Gesture`
**Category:** basic

A RotationGesture is emitted when the user moves two fingers in a circular motion.<br />     A rotation gesture will continue to be sent to the actor under the center point of the rotation until the rotation ends.<br />

## Properties

| Name | Type | Description |
|------|------|-------------|
| Rotation | `float` | A RotationGesture is emitted when the user moves two fingers in a circular motion.<br />     A rotation gesture will continue to be sent to the actor under the center point of the rotation until the rotation ends.<br /> |
| ScreenCenterPoint | `Vector2` | The center point of the two points that caused the rotation gesture in screen coordinates. |
| LocalCenterPoint | `Vector2` | The center point of the two points that caused the rotation gesture in local view coordinates. |
